# Data Processing Addendum (DPA)

Effective Date: [INSERT DATE]

This Data Processing Addendum ("DPA") forms part of the agreement between [COMPANY LEGAL NAME] ("Processor") and Customer ("Controller") for use of Proxaly.

## 1. Definitions

- Controller, Processor, Data Subject, Personal Data, Processing: meanings under applicable data protection laws.
- Customer Data: data submitted by Controller through the Service.

## 2. Scope and Roles

- Controller determines purposes and means of processing Customer Data.
- Processor processes Customer Data on documented instructions from Controller.

## 3. Processing Details

- Subject matter: provisioning and operation of SaaS prospecting and automation features.
- Duration: term of service agreement and post-termination retention window.
- Nature and purpose: storage, organization, retrieval, enrichment workflows, and communications orchestration.
- Data categories: business contact records and account metadata.
- Data subjects: customer users and business prospects/leads, as submitted by Controller.

## 4. Processor Obligations

Processor will:

- Process data only per documented instructions.
- Ensure confidentiality obligations for authorized personnel.
- Implement appropriate security measures.
- Assist Controller with data subject rights requests where feasible.
- Assist with security incident response obligations where required by law.
- Delete or return Customer Data upon termination, subject to legal retention obligations.

## 5. Subprocessors

Controller authorizes use of subprocessors necessary to provide the Service. Processor will maintain a subprocessors list and impose appropriate data protection obligations.

## 6. International Transfers

Where applicable, transfers are protected by appropriate safeguards (for example SCCs).

## 7. Security Incidents

Processor will notify Controller without undue delay after becoming aware of a confirmed personal data breach affecting Customer Data.

## 8. Audits and Information

Processor will provide information reasonably necessary to demonstrate compliance, subject to confidentiality and security constraints.

## 9. Liability

Liability under this DPA is subject to the limitations in the main agreement unless otherwise required by law.

## 10. Governing Terms

Except as modified by this DPA, the main agreement remains in effect.
